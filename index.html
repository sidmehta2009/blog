Avengers Endgame
you need to watch it 3-4 times to understand some concepts

Where to begin, where to begin! You know a movie is outstanding when the end credits alone are more epic than the majority of films released in the last 20 years! This film is the pure definition of an emotional roller coaster and throughout it's run time brings about fascination, humor, sadness, incredible excitement, and sheer finality. This is not your typical superhero movie and some viewers used to the entertaining, nonstop action style of the previous films may be disappointed, but I think that this movie brilliantly satisfies and subverts expectations.

Spiderman No Way Home
A good film for people who liked the old spiderman better

The reason Spiderman is the most popular MCU hero as reflected by not one not two but three movie trilogies has always been his idealistic, uncorrupted world view as a teenager and the relatable mistakes , naivete and teenage drama he consistently goes through. Tobey McGuire embodied this in the original series and Andrew Garfield lent his own distinct take to the character. But Tom Holland has truly elevated the web-slinger to another level (or should I say universe) with a stunning performance in No Way Home that is not just an immensely satisfying film for MCU fans but also great cinema.
